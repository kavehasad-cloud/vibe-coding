Who we are

Founded in Milan in 2004, Varenne & Partners is an independent advisory firm built on a simple conviction: the hardest problems are never solved from the outside.

Across more than 500 engagements we have worked shoulder-to-shoulder with the leaders of European industrials, consumer brands, and their investors — through growth, transition, and the occasional reinvention.

We are deliberately small. That is not a constraint; it is the design. Every engagement is led by a partner, staffed lean, and measured by one thing only: whether the decision we helped you make still looks right three years later.

How we work

  • Embedded.

    We work inside your business, not above it — in your meetings, your data rooms, your difficult Mondays.

  • Independent.

    We hold no product to sell and no answer prepared in advance. Our only loyalty is to the quality of the call.

  • Accountable.

    We measure ourselves the way you do: by results that compound long after the slides are closed.
