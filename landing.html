About

Small by design

Pieni Keittiö means "little kitchen," and that's exactly what we are. Tucked into a quiet corner of Punavuori, we serve a single changing menu built around what's fresh, local, and in season. No sprawling à la carte — just a handful of dishes we believe in that night.

Everything is made in-house, from the rye bread to the sea-buckthorn dessert. We keep the room intimate so every plate gets the attention it deserves, and so the people cooking are never far from the people eating.
